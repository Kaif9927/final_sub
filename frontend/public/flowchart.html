How it fits together

You sign in once, open Home, then use the links and tiles that match your role.

Events and your bookings. Admins also see Maintenance.
↓

/membership.html — accounts (any role) and subscriptions. /reports.html — numbers.
Vendor
Products, orders, and shipping updates under Vendor portal in the nav.
User
User portal and shop: vendors → products → cart → checkout.

APIs

REST under /api/... with a session cookie. Health check: GET /api/health. If the browser UI is on a different host or port than the API, set ALLOWED_ORIGINS in the backend .env (comma-separated URLs) so CORS and credentials work.
